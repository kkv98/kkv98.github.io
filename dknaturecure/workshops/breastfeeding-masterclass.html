Hi everyone! I am Dr. Dhivya Varshini, a Yoga and Naturopathy Physician and a Certified Lactation Counselor at DK Nature Cure Clinic.

In honor of World Breastfeeding Week (August 1st – 7th), we are hosting an exclusive and comprehensive Breastfeeding Masterclass. Whether you are expecting or already on your motherhood journey, this session is designed to give you the confidence, knowledge, and clinical support you need.

📚 What You Will Learn

Our intensive 2-hour expert-led session is structured around five key pillars of breastfeeding health:

1 Physiology of Breastfeeding

Understanding how your body naturally produces milk and responds to your baby's needs, biological triggers, and hormones.

2 Common Difficulties for Mother & Baby

Learning how to identify, troubleshoot, and navigate common nursing challenges (soreness, engorgement, blockages) smoothly.

3 Signs Your Baby is Getting Enough Milk

Simple clinical and practical indicators to give you peace of mind and confidence that your baby is well-fed.

4 Nutrition During Postpartum

Essential dietary guidelines and natural wellness tips to help your body recover while supporting a healthy, nutrient-rich milk supply.

5 Advanced Nursing Techniques

Step-by-step guidance on achieving a deep latch, pumping methods, hygienic milk storage, and hand expression techniques.
